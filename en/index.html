About us

We are a small family business owned by mother and daughter. We welcome you with love and pleasure. We want to have a restaurant with a fun and homely feel and food that’s been prepared with love and care with the best quality we can offer. Together with our fantastic colleagues who also care about the service and making sure that our guests have the best experience possible. We’re all “Pietje Peks” here.
